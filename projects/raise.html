Capstone Project: Raise

Raise is an Android app that connects users with actionable opportunities to get involved with causes they care about. Through the app, users can create and explore events, fundraisers, and groups which champion a variety of causes, ranging from conservation to LGBT rights.

The concept, user interface, and user experience were researched and designed by Bonnie Sweet. I was the sole engineer.

I had three weeks to learn all the technologies involved (I had no experience in any of them) and build the app.

Technologies used: Kotlin, Android Studio, Google Firebase, Google Firestore, Google Maps & Google Places APIs.
